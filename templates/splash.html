THE IDEA

We make when to go easy

You want to go on vacation with your family or friends. But you don't know when everyone is available, and it is just to darn tough to figure it out out of the 365 possible days. We are here to help !

You and your travel buddies will create a "group" on the account. This will allow us to sync your calendars and find the perfect time to vacation. In addition you will have some access to some tools that will help narrow down your perfect travel time such as weather predictions, cheap flights, and others !

THE TEAM

Some Kids Who Want To Vacay

We are a team of summer Austin interns that wanted to create a tool to help people get out on vacation easier with their family and friends.
